Chocolate Sheet Cake

Cake Ingredients:
Step One: Step Two: Step Three:
1 Stick of Butter 2 Cups of Flour 2 Eggs
3 TBSP Cocoa 1/2 TSP Salt 1/2 Cup Buttermilk
1/2 Cup Oil 2 Cups Sugar 1 TSP Vanilla
1 Cup Water 1 TSP Baking Soda
Icing Ingredients:
Step One: Step Two:
1 Stick of Butter 1 Pound Powdered Sugar
1 TBSP Cocoa 1 TSP Vanilla
6 TBSP Buttermilk Pinch of Salt
Cake Instructions:
Spray half sheet pan with non-stick spray.
Bring Step One ingredients to a boil.
Pour over Step Two ingredients.
Add Step Three ingredients, mix well.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
Icing Instructions:
Bring Step One ingredients to a boil.
Pour over powdered sugar.
Add remaining Step Two ingredients, mix well.
Pour over hot cake. Let cool before eating.
