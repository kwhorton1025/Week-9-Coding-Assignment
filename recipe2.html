Angel Biscuits

Ingredients:
Step One: Step Two:
1 Package of Instant Yeast 3/4 Cup Butter Crisco 1 TSP Baking Powder
1/2 Cup of Warm Water 2 TSP Salt 2 Cups Whole Milk
1/3 Cup Sugar 5 Cups All-Purpose Flour
Instructions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water, set aside.
Measure out Step Two ingredients. Cut Crisco in with pastry cutter.
Mix in with yeast and warm water. Place in refrigerator over night.
Knead on floured surface and roll out, cut out with biscuit cutter.
Place biscuits on baking sheet to rise for one hour.
Bake at 450 degrees until golden brown.
